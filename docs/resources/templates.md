These starter templates help teams turn guidance into reusable requests and records. Adapt them to the product, sector, lifecycle stage, and assurance decision.

## Supplier Evidence Request

Use with [Procurement & Supplier Assurance](../practices-controls/procurement-supplier-assurance.md), [Supplier Questions](./supplier-questions.md), and the [Evidence Checklist](./evidence-checklist.md).

```text
Decision:
Lifecycle stage:
Risk or failure mode:
Practice or control:
Evidence requested:
Required artifact format:
Producer or owner:
Verification path:
Retention expectation:
Weak answer:
Better answer:
Stronger answer:
Open gaps:
```

## Product Acceptance Record

Use with [Product Acceptance](../practices-controls/product-acceptance.md), [Identity & Provenance](../evidence/identity-provenance.md), and [Integrity & Attestation](../evidence/integrity-attestation.md).

```text
Product or platform:
Supplier:
Acceptance decision:
Identity evidence reviewed:
Provenance evidence reviewed:
Integrity evidence reviewed:
Transparency artifacts reviewed:
Update and vulnerability records reviewed:
Lifecycle-state evidence reviewed:
Verifier or reviewer:
Decision:
Conditions or remediation actions:
Evidence retention location:
```

## Standards Mapping Record

Standard, framework, or technology:
