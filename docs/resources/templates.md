These starter templates help teams turn guidance into reusable requests and records. Adapt them to the product, sector, lifecycle stage, and assurance decision.

## Supplier Evidence Request

Use with [Supplier & Procurement Assurance](../practices-controls/supplier-procurement-assurance.md), [Supplier Questions](./supplier-questions.md), and the [Evidence Checklist](./evidence-checklist.md).

```text
Decision:
Lifecycle stage:
Risk or failure mode:
Practice or control:
Evidence requested:
Required artifact format:
Producer or owner:
Verification path:
Retention expectation:
Weak answer:
Better answer:
Stronger answer:
Open gaps:
```

